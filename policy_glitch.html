Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Glitch Dash mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection

As the developers of Glitch Dash, we do not directly collect, store, transmit, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

All game progress, high scores, unlocked skins, and coin balances are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your personal gameplay data.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data directly, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information to identify you, primarily for advertising and app improvement purposes.

These third parties may collect data logically associated with your device, including your device's IP address, advertising ID (such as Apple's IDFA or Google's Advertising ID), operating system version, and usage patterns.

We use the following third-party service providers in our App:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. This helps us keep the game free for everyone.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Used for processing In-App Purchases (Pro version) and managing game downloads.

For more detailed information on how these third-party services handle your data, please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

3. Opt-Out Rights

You can opt-out of personalized advertising collection by changing the privacy settings on your mobile device:

  • iOS Devices: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and adjust the "Allow Apps to Request to Track" setting.
  • Android Devices: Go to Settings > Privacy > Ads, and select "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete advertising ID".

Additionally, you can stop all data collection by simply uninstalling the App from your device.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
